Jewellery can be an extremely personal affair; it can hold a million emotions in it especially if it is for some one very special. We at Jzeva, would feel special to be a part of your personal journey of discovering your style and personality and inducing it in your jewellery.
How to go about your bespoke journey?
The Journey starts with you booking an appointment with our Concierge services team.
We will get one of our Elite designers to talk to you on the booked appointment date and time.
The designer will understand your ideas, thoughts and dreams about your design.
The designer will produce a sketch of the ideas and get your approval (multiple iterations may be required).
We will also help you choose the right precious metal and gemstones for the design.
Once the design is approved a computer generated 3D model will be shared with you which will give you close enough feel of the actual live look.
Once you approve the render, we will manufacture, certify and Hallmark the jewellery.
A final round our quality check from our side and your bespoke jewellery is ready to be shipped.
Why Bespoke Jewellery?
The bespoke jewellery you spend time designing and bringing to life is your style and personality induced in it, and it will be as unique as you are as there will be no piece that will be the same as the one you have designed.
I already have a design in mind!
Brilliant! If sketching is your thing, go ahead and share the sketch with us and we will take over from there.
Are there any pre-requisites?
If you need a designer to work with you personally to design your bespoke jewellery, the order size needs to be a minimum of INR 3,00,000.
If you have a design in mind and can share the sketch of it, you can get your personalised piece of jewellery starting from INR 25,000.
