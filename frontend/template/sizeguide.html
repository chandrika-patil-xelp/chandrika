Sizing Guide
99% of the jewellery buyers will not be able to recall their ring size and buying the right size is one of the most important things to consider while buying a ring, too tight it will restrict blood flow and cause a lot of inconvenience too loose it will just slip out of your finger. At Jzeva we realise this and want to ensure that you do not make a mistake.
At Jzeva there are 2 ways of finding out what your ring size is.
Option 1 (and the best one)
After finalising your ring design and selecting the size select the “Not Sure” option and go ahead with the payment as soon as you place your order we will send you a Jzeva Ring sizer which should reach you in 2-3 working days, once you receive it all you need to do is measure your ring size and tell us and we will take care of the rest. Yes!!! Its is that simple.
Option 2 (almost as good as option 1)
Take one of the rings that you already own, ensure that I fits you well.
Take a scale and measure the diameter (the largest distance) of the inner edges of the ring.
Once you know the diameter compare it to the chart below to find your ring size.

Option 3 ( not so accurate but will do)
Take a thin strip of paper not more than 0.5 cm in width and 10 cm in length.
Wrap it around the finger you want to wear the ring on.
Take a pen and mark the meeting point.
Measure the distance between the meeting points with a ruler, this will give you the circumference of your finger.
Use the chart below to determine your ring size.
