warranty and repair
warranty
Every Piece of Jzeva jewellery comes with a warranty cover of 24 months from the date of purchase for normal wear and tear related issues.
considered normal wear and tear
Loss of sheen
Loss of rhodium coating
Loose diamonds or gemstones
Services for the items covered under the warranty will be provided free of cost including the shipping.
not considered as normal wear and tear and hence not covered under warranty
Scratches or impression created due to external forces
Loss of diamonds or gemstones
Loss of original shape and form of the jewellery
Cracks in diamonds or gemstones.
Theft or loss of jewellery.
Though the above mentioned items are not covered under warranty Jzeva will still provide repair services to rectify the jewellery, the customer will be charged a fee based on the service provided and for the to and fro shipping of the product.
Repairs
Even the best of thing require you to take care of them with great attention and care, but its only natural for things to as not planned. In the unfortunate case that your jewellery needs repairs just call our customer satisfaction team and we will take care of it. The repair services will be done at a nominal and transparent service fee and the shipping fee to get your jewellery to it past glory.
