Bangle Sizing Guide
Finding the right size of a bangle might be a little bit of a confusing affair, should I measure the size of my palm or should I measure the size of my wrist? Well, read on and do as we say, you will be done finding out your bangle size in under 2 mins.
Option 1 (the most accurate way)
Find a bangle that you already have and can wear it with ease and leave it on comfortably
Take a ruler and measure the diameter (the largest distance between the inner edges).
Note it down and compare it to the chart below to find out your bangle size.

Option 2
Take a thin strip of paper not more the 0.5cm wide and 15 cm in length.
Now bring in all your finger together to meet at the tip of your thumb.
Take the strip of paper and wrap it around the widest part of your palm.
Take a pen and mark the meeting point of the paper.
Measure the distance between the two marks with a ruler.
Note it down and compare it with the chart below to find out your bangle size.
