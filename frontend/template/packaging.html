Luxury Packaging
Luxury is consistently meeting and exceeding your customer’s expectations, and we at Jzeva believe in giving a complete 360° luxurious experience. A piece of art needs to be taken care of, stored properly and needs to be as good as new every time you look at it. Jzeva’s Luxury packaging just aims to do the same. We have specially designed out packaging keeping in mind the requirements to safe guard our jewellery and ensure that they look and feel the same way as it did the last time you wore it irrespective of the time gap.
Jewellery Box
Every piece of jewellery you buy from Jzeva is delivered to you in a luxurious leatherette box, with appropriate and subtle branding on it. The box is easy to store, easy to handle and easy to carry (if need be).
Contents
The jewellery box will contain you Jzeva jewellery and Certificate of Authenticity hence you have all that you need to take care of your jewellery in box.
Exterior Packaging
Your Jzeva jewellery will be shipped to you in a tamperproof, bubble wrapped and secure exterior packaging. The only way to get the jewellery out of this packing once it is sealed is by ripping the cover apart, hence you can be sure that you will be able recognise even if it tampered a tiny bit.
