returns and exchange
return an item?
Want to return an item you purchase from us? No Problem!!!! We will accept it as long as it fulfils the below criteria.
The value of the jewellery you purchased is below ₹30,000 in value.
The return is initiated within 7 days from the order delivery date.
The tamper proof tag on the jewellery delivered to you is not be removed, damaged or tampered with.
The jewellery should not show any signs of wear and tear or damage.
In the event the jewellery you wish to return fails to meet the above criteria, the jewellery will be shipped back to the customer.
Refunds
You can opt for a refund on the items that have been returned to us either as store credit or to your original payment method. The following conditions will apply.
Refunds will be initiated only on successful fulfilment of the returns criteria.
If refund is requested in Store Credits no deductions what so ever will be made.
If the refund is requested as cash, cheque or account transfer, a 10% of the cost of the product will be deducted towards making, certification and return shipping costs.
If the jewellery is returned due to a defect caused from our side, we will refund 100% of your invoice value.
Any Discounts resulting from promo codes applied during purchase shall not be refunded.
Refunds for purchases made through gift cards or vouchers will be credited back to the gift card or voucher.
Refunds will be processed in 10-15 days from the date of request.
Cancellation
Placed an order but had a change of mind later? If it’s not too late you can still exercise the cancellation option.
Order Confirmed: 100% refund
Quality check and certification: 7% of the order value will be deducted
Shipped: No cancellation option
